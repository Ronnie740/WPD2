AGB Wellness
Program

The company is committed to your health and happiness, not just at work but in your personal life too. To help achieve this goal we have setup a program of activities so you can keep healthy and have fun here at work. We may even provide some goodies from time to time to help you out. Please take the time to read through the wellness plan and familiarise yourself with our goals and how we are going to meet them. We look forward to working together towards healthier happier lives.

Fitness

AGB is a company that's always on the lookout for new ways to make our employees healthier and happier. We thinks that exercise should be fun, and we want to make sure that our staff have a way to enjoy it outside of their own homes. In order to do this, we have created a fitness program called "AGB-Active," which encourages employees to get active in their workday by providing them with equipment for aerobics classes and yoga sessions every day.

Nutrition

Nutrition is an important part of a healthy lifestyle. It's important that we make sure we're getting the right nutrients and vitamins in our diet, so we can feel good and function at our best.

Mental Health

AGB has an Employee Wellness Program that includes monthly wellness check-ins, a corporate yoga studio, meditation classes, and access to a private counselor who specializes in workplace issues. We also offer regular online workshops on topics ranging from stress management to mindfulness meditation, which can be very helpful if you're looking for ways to improve your mental health at work as well as outside of it.
